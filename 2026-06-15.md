## 🧠 Improvements & Learnings

📌 **New Rules / Discoveries**
- **In-Memory Optimization Efficiency**: Downloading raw stock metrics and holding returns exactly once, then evaluating candidates in-memory, speeds up optimization by thousands of times and eliminates API rate limiting risks.
- **Weights Summation Constraint**: When perturbing weights during coordinate descent, normalize the array to ensure they consistently sum to 1.0.
