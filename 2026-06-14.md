## 🧠 Improvements & Learnings

📌 **New Rules / Discoveries**
- **Domain-Specific Constraints**: Financial institutions (banks) must bypass cash flow and gross margin analysis because normal operating items (customer deposits and loans) make their cash flow reports volatile and non-comparable to industrials.
- **Pydantic Declaration Order**: In Pydantic v2, referencing a class as a type hint or default factory before it is declared raises a `NameError` at runtime during module loading unless resolved.

🔄 **Optimizations & Reflections**
- Refactored rigid linear scaling boundaries into dynamic sector-aware configurations, making it extremely easy to extend scanner templates to new industries.
